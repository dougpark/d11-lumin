Custom Short Links

Public & Private

How It Works

Three steps to a smarter link workflow.

1

Save a URL

Paste any URL into d11.me. Add a title, tags, and an optional description to keep things organized.

2

Choose a Slug

Give it a short name like cowboys and it becomes d11.me/l/cowboys instantly.

3

Share & Redirect

Anyone who visits your short link is instantly forwarded to the full destination URL in their browser.
